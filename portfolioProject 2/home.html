Introduction


Hello, I'm Emily, a student at Wake Technical Community College on an exciting journey into the world of tech. My path wasn't always set on this course—originally pursuing studies in radiology. However, when I met my fiancé, a software developer, I was intrigued by his work. I found myself drawn into the possibilities within the tech industry. In 2022, I made the decision to pivot my studies, transitioning from Radiology to Web & UX Design. I beagan learning HTML, CSS, and PHP. Next came design fundamentals and the principles of user-centered design. During the summer of 2023 I started learning JavaScript, expanding my toolkit further. I then decided to pursue a dual degree, now balancing studies in Web Development alongside Web & UX Design. I am currently learning C#, navigating Adobe Creative Applications, and exploring social media and digital marketing. I'm happy to say that I am expected to graduate Spring 2025. On this site, I hope to share my skills as well as my growth with you. I hope you can see improvement in my skills along the way.
